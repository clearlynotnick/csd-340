Chapter 2: Getting Personal

I was born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky. While many other places around the area of Kentucky were harder to explain, it became quite easy to elaborate where I was born from to others from around the country. All I had to do was say, "you know where they race horses at? Yeah, there." While I consider it my hometown, I do not necessarily consider it "home" to me anymore.

There isn't much to say about my childhood at all. I was born lower-middle class and it had the usual story you here - the family struggled, I wanted a better life for myself whenever I got older, so on and so forth. I had many different passions growing up including music, creating content on YouTube, and doing graphic design. While I was never perfect at any one thing, each skill grew more and more as I worked harder at each dicipline.

My love for everything creative has spanned every since then. Nowadays I love to go out and take photos, edit videos (I do a lot of work with weddings), and expand my own personal experiences. Traveling is something I always wanted to do growing up and I'm so happy with how many different areas I've traveled to now! There are so many new places I want to see still including Austria, Italy, and Norway.

I am incredibly happy with where I have gotten to in my life. While it isn't necessarily perfect (I hate student loans), I can say without a shadow of doubt that I have done so much more than I ever thought possible. I would eventually like a family one day but at this current stage in life I would like to become better with my discipline, my work ethic, and also live a bit more until I family decide to have a family.
